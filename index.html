My design process

I follow to a set of processes that enable me to assure both user-friendly design and business success. My typical method is outlined here, however it may vary depending on the needs of the project.

Empathise

In first stage, I get clear about who actual user is, what they want, and which challenges and problems they facing. In addition business goals, and technical obstacles.

Define actual problem

Using previous research findings and business requirements, I identify the actual problems. It is important to define the actual problem before starting to design a solution.

Design to produce solutions

Once the problems is identified the I jump into make multiple solutions to achieve goals. Sometimes its lo-fi wireframe sometimes mid or high-fi wireframe.

Prototype

A prototype is a working model of a product or system that is used to test and refine the design. create a prototype to show stakeholders how it will work in real time.

Test against requirements

I test the design to ensure that it meets the needs of the end users as well as the business requirements.
